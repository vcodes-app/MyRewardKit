About

RewardKit App

As a parent, I am deeply troubled by the the fact that my kid seems to be addicted to the screens, phone, TV, iPad, computer, you name it. No matter how I restrict his screen time, It doesn't seeem very effective. Especially that, even when I remove the screens, I failed to channel his energy or motivate him to do other things.

Things that do not gratify him instantly, things that need long time efforts to build to see any effect, things that are not easy and even painful for adults.

Tried shouting, yelling, reasoning, anything. But they are not long term solutions. And I definitely don't like to turn myself into a tiger parent or a monster for that matter. Then one day I decided to have an App, that can help me keep tracks of tasks given and the rewards the kids can claim from completing these tasks and let's just call it A win-win reward system.

This is how I usually apply it to our schedule. Every weekend, we make a deal of what is required to be done next week. And for each task completed, a reward can be claimed, but game type of rewards can only be claimed at the weekend. Some of the typical tasks look like:

  • Daily Practise Drum: 30 minutes => Game: 10 minutes
  • Every Tuesday English reading: 60 minutes => iphone: 30 minutes
  • Every Thursday Spelling quiz practice: 60 minutes => Game: 20 minutes

Summary - Friday Night: game x 60 minutes

So far it works out pretty good. Thank GOD! Hope it works for you too! CONTACT: app.vcodes@gmail.com
